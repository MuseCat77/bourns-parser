Wirewound Resistors

Home / Products / Resistors / Wirewounds
Specifications
Quick link to the Sample Request: 

Bourns offers a broad range of wirewound resistors with axial leaded through hole and surface mounted packages. These resistors are made by wrapped a high-performance resistive alloy wire around a low thermal resistance ceramic core. Wirewound resistors have superior pulse capability and available in non-inductive styles as well.

W, WS and FW series axial leaded through hole wirewound resistors are coated by UL 94V-0 compliant material.

FW series axial fusible resistor is UL1412 recognized and offers an integrated solution as it provides both inrush current protection and fusible overcurrent protection.

PWR series resistors covered by epoxy molded encapsulation which provides environmental protection for the resistive wire. And due to their wraparound terminations, they are suitable for surface mounting technology.

  • High power rating

  • Customized parts (surge capability, lead form)
